# InfraBuild Partners Brand Memory

## Positioning
InfraBuild Partners is a hybrid technical-services and career-acceleration company. The public offer spans:
- Engineering services
- Bootcamp / guided learning
- Interview preparation
- Job search and placement support

## Current Website Offer Structure
- Engineering:
  - Development Support
  - DevOps Support
  - Bootcamp Service
  - SRE Support
  - Data Engineering
  - Data Analyst
  - Interview Support
- Career:
  - Job Applying Support
  - Resume & LinkedIn Optimization
  - Full Job Search Management
  - End-to-End Placement Support

## Go-To-Market Priorities
- LinkedIn:
  - Founder-led authority posts
  - Case-study carousels
  - Hiring / training opportunity posts
- Instagram:
  - Short educational reels
  - Offer explainers
  - Social-proof snippets
- WhatsApp:
  - Channel for one-way broadcast updates
  - Business chat or API flow for lead capture and candidate screening

## Content Themes
- DevOps and SRE credibility
- Real client delivery stories
- Career acceleration and job support
- Bootcamp outcomes and practical implementation
- Infrastructure reliability, cloud, and engineering execution

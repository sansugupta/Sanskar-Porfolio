- Keep the existing digital-agency service catalog.
- Add a new section such as `Technical Delivery & Career Support`.
- Separate "Devodus Digital Services" from "Powered Delivery Services".
- Use data-driven service files so pricing is easy to update without editing HTML.
- Add lead capture forms with clear service routing:
  - Devodus design/marketing inquiries
  - InfraBuild delivery/job-support/bootcamp inquiries
- Preserve Devodus brand/contact details until the client provides updated official details.
